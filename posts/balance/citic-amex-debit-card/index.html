Amex & Citic Debit Card (CNY)

The joint venture between American Express and LianLian Group, called LianTong, obtained the Bank Card Clearing License from the People’s Bank of China in mainland China in August 2020. It is currently the first Sino-foreign joint venture bank card clearing institution in China.

Reference Amex announces first RMB debit cards

This article is about the “American Express® Gold Debit Card” issued by China Citicbank starting from June 8th.

This is a card that even China Citicbank’s counter staff are not familiar with and their first reaction is that it is a credit card…

The card is made of plastic and does not have the “Member Since” number. Since it is a CNY account, it must be a type 1 or 2 card.

It starts with the first six digits number: 377138. Let’s look up some information about it.

By linking your card to the American Express WeChat official account, you can view the list of privileges.

To summarize:

  • The first three overseas ATM withdrawals each month are exempt from transaction fees and currency conversion fees.
  • The card can be linked to WeChat (although I was not successful in doing so) and Alipay.
  • The first 2,000 activated cards will receive a $5 American Express red packet for the first three transactions made on Alipay.

I was actually one of the first 2,000 activated cards, hahaha.

The 9.9 yuan and 5 yuan red packet promotion from Luckin Coffee is really enjoyable.
